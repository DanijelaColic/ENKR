Why Osijek businesses need a website

Slavonia is going through a digital transformation. The businesses that adapt first — win.

Osijek — IT hub of Eastern Croatia

Over the past decade, Osijek has emerged as a significant IT hub. FERIT faculty and the Software Park attract young talent, while companies in the IT Park prove that Slavonia can compete globally. This digital energy is spreading to traditional businesses — bakeries, car repair shops, family farms and family restaurants.

However, many local entrepreneurs still don't have a website or use outdated templates from 2015. While a customer in Donji grad searches "hairdresser Osijek" on Google, your competitor with a first-result listing is already receiving a call.
