Personal data may include your first name, last name, email address, and other information that can be used to identify you.

2.2 Blog and Comments

If you leave a comment on our blog, we store:

  • name

Comments remain published until either the comment or the blog post is removed. To delete your comment, you may contact us via the email address used when posting the comment.
